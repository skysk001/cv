Who Am I ?

About Me

Hello! I am Sujeet Kumar Yadav, a passionate and dedicated professional with a diploma in Computer Science, awarded in 2019. I bring over four years of experience in the dynamic field of web development, specializing in PHP, Laravel, Node.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ajax, Bootstrap, and MySQL.
Throughout my career, I have actively contributed to a diverse range of projects, showcasing my versatility as both a backend and frontend developer. My expertise extends to API development.
I have had the privilege of working on various projects, including those for NGOs, educational institutions, delivery services, administrative panels, blogs, CRMs, LMS, and informational websites. This diverse experience has equipped me with a holistic understanding of web development and a keen ability to tailor solutions to meet specific project requirements.
I am excited about the prospect of bringing my technical expertise and innovative mindset to a new and challenging role. Let's connect and explore how I can contribute to the success of your team and projects.
